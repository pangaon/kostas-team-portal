# Kostas Meat Market — quick guide

## Your live app
**https://kostas-team-portal.vercel.app**

- Coach sign-in: tap **Coach sign in** → your email + password.
- Add to your phone: browser **Share → Add to Home Screen** (works like an app, and now it reopens straight to your dashboard).

## The ONE message to send parents (copy-paste into WhatsApp)

> Hi everyone! 👋 We've set up a team app for Kostas Meat Market this season — schedule, attendance, snack signup, carpool, and game-day info all in one place (no more lost WhatsApp messages).
>
> Tap this link on your phone to get set up — it takes 1 minute:
> https://kostas-team-portal.vercel.app/join/kostas-meat-market-2U2F
>
> 👉 Your child is already on the roster — just **tap their name** to join (or "Add a new player" if they're not listed).
> Then tap **Add to Home Screen** and turn on alerts so you get game reminders.
>
> See you at the field! — Coach George

## What parents can do
Mark attendance (Going / Maybe / Can't), get one-tap directions to the field, claim a snack date, sign up for carpool, vote on practice times, message you directly, and get push alerts when you post.

## What you (coach) can do
Dashboard, Roster (approve/add/edit), Schedule, **Lineup** (8-a-side: 7 + GK), **Season** (scores, record, top scorers), Attendance, Snacks, **Poll** (find a practice time), **Inbox** (parent notes), Announcements (push to all), Messages (WhatsApp templates), Game-day check-in & printable sheet, Settings.
